About Me

Hello!

My name is Vincent Young, and I'm a Biomedical Engineering graduate from the University of Waterloo (Class of 2019), working in the field of display image processing, with a focus on algorithms pertaining to OLED panel processing, image scaling, and color management.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to design and develop new and exciting products with talented teams from Apple, Axon, Synaptive Medical, and Baylis Medical. I'm always looking for interesting projects to work on, and look forward to see what I'll have a chance to collaborate on next!

Some of the things I enjoy doing include hiking, cooking new foods (and eating them of course!), listening to podcasts, and meeting new people while traveling to places I've never been before.

To learn more about me, please take a look at my résumé and my LinkedIn.
